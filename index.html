About Diana's Jewellery...

The first milestone in the company's philosophy was the production of gold and precious jewels. Thanks to the know-how acquired in the last thirty years in jewelry production, we have been aiming to follow the quality standards even better and to bring them together with visionary customers in jewelery stores, where jewelry is represented on almost every continent or in various cities. With Diana Jewelry, we decided to make a difference in the world of jewelery.

Diana Jewelry, aiming to share its 30 years of experience with its valued customers, created its own showcase in this small and beautiful city of Montenegro against this unique ground. Visit us and enjoy the unforgettable moments of this world as a partner in sparkling and fun in our own world. We are pleased to present our knowledge of gold and precious stone jewelry, and our long-standing knowledge of our diverse and first-class selections.
